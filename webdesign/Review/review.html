CustomerReview

Nazmul Huda

A good option in Happy Food. I and my friend had dinner here one of the nights we were in Happy Food. The ambiance was good and the food recommendations were great. We had a traditional Arabian rice and chicken preparation, Kuzhi Manthi. It was our first experience having this dish, and although a bit dry, it tasted great. Would recommend this place that's easily noticed from a vehicle and has ample parking.

Sumiya Benta Shihave

Good place to eat at reasonable cost! We visited this place for breakfast and dinner. The place is very conveniently located (on the highway ) with ample parking space. The food at both the meals was tasty and definitely worth the price. Good quantity served per portion. Service as also good. Overall , a good place to have food (both local area and highway) when staying in Happy Food. Would recommend it definitely.
